Who We Are

Established in September 2000, #Genesis is a Zambian-registered company specializing in the distribution of networking equipment and accessories for LAN and WAN environments.

We are part of the Genesis Group of Companies, with operations across Southern Africa. Our goal is to deliver quality ICT products and reliable service backed by a skilled and dedicated team with direct links to global manufacturers.
